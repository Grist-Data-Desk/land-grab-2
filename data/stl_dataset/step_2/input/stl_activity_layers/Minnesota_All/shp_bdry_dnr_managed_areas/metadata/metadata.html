Section 1: Overview

Originator:Minnesota Department of Natural Resources (DNR) - Fisheries Division

Title: State Administered Lands - DNR Management Units, Minnesota

Abstract: This data layer shows lands acquired, administered and/or managed by Minnesota DNR. These include lands in the following categories:
  • Aquatic Management Area (AMA)

  • Scientific and Natural Area (SNA)

  • State Forest

  • Other Forest Land

  • State Park

  • State Recreation Area

  • State Wayside

  • Wildlife Management Area (WMA)

Management Units are often composed of numerous tracts of land that are managed or administered as a single unit.

Some features in this layer are hyperlinked to websites or documents that provide descriptive information. Use ArcMap's Hyperlink Tool (yellow lightning bolt) to access deed documents. (To set up the hyperlink, open Layer Properties: Display tab, check the Hyperlinks box and choose the field [WEB_LINK1] or [WEB_LINK2].)

Purpose: For more information about management units, see the following:
Classification and Purposes - MN Statutes 86A: http://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/?id=86A.05
